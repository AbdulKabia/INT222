Profile

Throughout my life thus far, I have actually come to learn many different styles of coding and many different languages to code in, such as HTML/CSS, PHP, JavaScript, JQuery, C, C++ and many more. My love for coding started in a very simple, yet very educational language known as Turing. Turing is the very first language I learnt in the 9th grade; it is what sparked my love for coding. It helped put together the building blocks of my understanding of code and how different segments of code interact together to create programs.

After graduating from Seneca, I look to further my studies by transferring over to York University in order to achieve a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. It is with that degree that I look to pursue a job at Google, actually. Google is a company that has a great infrastructure; their employees get paid a great amount and are provided with great health care and other benefits. One of the reasons why I want to work for Google is also because of where the job I am looking for is stationed at. I’m currently not happy with being in Canada; I actually want to move out of here and work at Google’s headquarters located in San José, California. The position that I will be going for when I apply is Software Engineer. After graduating from York, I believe that I will have achieved sufficient knowledge to apply for such a position at such a company and be able to come out successful.

However, things are not always as planned in life. So if the dream of working at Google is not one that will be successful, I will be looking for another path, perhaps one as a teacher. I will, of course, be teaching Computer Science or something related to IT. I have always believed that I would be a great teacher; I believe I will do a much better job than most of the teachers I have had in the past!

So that’s enough about me and my aspirations. Simply put, I’m nothing more than a man with a dream of becoming a great programmer and doing wonderful things with the knowledge and experience I will come to achieve in my lifetime.
