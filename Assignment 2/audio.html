Audio implementation in HTML5

A new feature in HTML5 allows developers to have audio files play on their websites. It is a very useful feature to use if you wish to have some audio available to your visitors, like a musical piece, or anything you would like, really.

This piece is called Bagatelle No. 25, but commonly known as "Für Elise", which is in English translates to "For Elise". It was published in 1867 after it's author, Ludwig van Beethoven had died in 1827.
